# TATA Online Retail Store Dashboard

##  Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing online retail store data to uncover key business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends.

---

##  Objective

To analyze transactional data and derive actionable insights that can help improve revenue, optimize inventory, and support business decision-making.

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* **Total Quantity Sold:** 5,176,450
* **Total Revenue:** ₹97.47 Lakhs

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🌍 Country Performance

* United Kingdom is the top revenue contributor (~₹8.2M)
* Other countries contribute significantly less revenue
* Revenue is highly concentrated in a single geography

---

### 📈 Sales Trends

* Peak sales observed in Month 11 (~0.74M units)
* Sharp drop in Month 12 (~0.23M units)
* Indicates strong seasonality in demand

---

### 📅 Yearly Growth

* 2011 sales (~4.8M units) significantly higher than 2010 (~0.3M units)
* Demonstrates rapid business growth

---

### 📊 Quarterly Analysis

* Q4 shows highest sales (~2M units)
* Q1 has the lowest sales (~1M units)
* Confirms strong year-end demand spike

---

### 🛒 Product Insights

* Sales are dominated by a few key products
* Top contributors include:

  * WORLD WAR items
  * JUMBO BAG
  * ASSORTED products

---

## Business Insights

* Business is heavily dependent on the UK market
* Strong seasonal demand during Q4
* A small number of products drive most revenue
* Significant opportunity to expand in other countries

---

## Recommendations

* Diversify revenue sources beyond the UK
* Optimize inventory for peak Q4 demand
* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products
* Target low-performing regions for expansion
